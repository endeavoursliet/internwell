About Us

ABC enterprises is an SME fighting hard against it's competitors to make a stand in the market. The biggest challenge is to gain the support it needs to win. InternWell has a solution for them as it brings out it's biggest weapons, the talented interns who'll establish it's digital presence in the market using the various skills at their ease. Through digitization, the sales and popularity of ABC enterprises soars to the sky. The interns from different universities got paid, worked on a live project that they can brag about, and most importantly, developed their skillset. But wait! There’s more! The money earned through this deal would not only be utilised for the company but will be spent to develop the technical skills of underprivileged people thus increasing their employability. This is exactly how InternWell works. We take a project from SMEs, hire undergrads as interns to work on the projects, and use the money earned in three different ways. We pay the interns with some money, utilise the funds for development of the company and also use it to develop the skills of the underprivileged so as to employ them or help them get employed in some other company. Our main focus is to help potential SMEs grow their trade by making complete use of digital services available to them and help students and underprivileged people upskill hence increasing their employability

InternWell brings to you 18-SBC, an 18 – day online course that will help you shape your dreams into reality. The nation is currently on lockdown but don’t worry, the internet and electricity are still active. Many students wait for a time when no one will stop them from pursuing their passion. Well, now the time has come, the time has come to drift from that monotonous academic curriculum and learn something interesting, let’s not waste this time. 18-SBC is a fun filled active course dedicated towards development of the skills that you can boast about. It includes interactive sessions from Prateek Narang – Cofounder Coding blocks, Dr. Manpreet Manna – Former Director AICTE and Founder of MHRD’s Swayam App, Blessin George – Google India’s AI/ML facilitator and many more brains that will help you hone your skills. For a more detailed list, go to the “Speaker Details” section. There is a small commitment fee for this course that will be later provided to the organisations that are supporting the poor to recover from the aftermath of the CoVID-19 Pandemic.

The Course will be held over a time span of 18 days during the pandemic. More details about the schedule will be updated soon. The course will be conducted on “Zoom” platform. The assignments will be given through Google classroom (or) Slack. Any discussions and questions will be resolved through Whatsapp groups. There will be credits for attending the lectures of the topics you choose and you shall be eligible for a certificate and other perks if you have minimum number of required credits.
